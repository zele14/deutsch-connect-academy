Our Services

At Deutsch Connect Academy, we offer a variety of services to meet the diverse needs of our students:

  • German Language Courses: Comprehensive courses from beginner (A1) to advanced (C2), focusing on all language skills.
  • Private Tutoring: Personalized sessions for targeted support in grammar, conversation, or exam preparation.
  • Business German: Specialized courses for mastering business communication and terminology in a German-speaking work environment.
  • Exam Preparation: Courses for official exams like Goethe-Zertifikat, TestDaF, and TELC, helping students build confidence and strategies for success.
